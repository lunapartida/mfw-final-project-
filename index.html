ABOUT

LUNAVIZION is a part of you and me. It is the VIZIONS we see and wish to create but don’t know where to start. It is the hope of the future and the goals we wish to create for ourselves, without boundaries, no limits, nothing to hold us back. WE REACH FOR THE STARS AND THE MOON AND WE STAY UP THERE! Hello, My name is Luna Partida! I’m a creative who likes to try as much as she can and here are some of my favorite things I’ve been up to lately: Graphic Design Website Development Sewing/Customizing Clothes Creating Fun DIY Projects and doing anything that involves creativity and brain power makes my mind and soul so happy! That’s why when I heard about BitWise’s classes, I had to give it a try! I was a MySpace kid who would customize mine and my friend’s pages and didn’t know I was using html! It was something I really enjoyed, and I’ve always wanted to come back to it! I was a kid who had a rough upbringing and it continued into my adult life and I always felt like I never had a chance because all the abuse I went through. In 2019 I was finally diagnosed with with CPTSD, severe depression and anxiety because of everything that I’ve gone through. I’ve been working really hard on trying to heal those issues because unfortunately I know it’s something I’m going to have to live with every day. I would love to be an inspiration for others who have been in similar situations as me so they can know they can get through anything that is thrown at them no matter how hard, hurtful and difficult it might be, to believe in themselves and to never give up! My dream is to become a video game developer and do some graphic design work at the same time while being able to stream the games I’m working on/have worked on and help educate and inspire other to follow their tech dreams as well as being an advocate for mental illness. I’d love to start some type of website or organization that advocates for mental health wellbeing in the tech industry. I appreciate you taking the time to read through my story and would love to hear from you if you have any questions, want to work together on some projects/collaborate or if you need advice, I’m always here! Thank you so much.
